Welcome to the Plant Walk

Here, plants become characters with voices, moods, and playlists of their own. From the bluesy resilience of the baldcypress to the dramatic flair of the wisteria and the soulful vibrancy of coleus, each mix invites you to experience plants in a new, symbiotic way where listening becomes connection, and every song deepens our bond with the living world.
